Acquisition Page Design

UX design and developing of a high-traffic acquisition page with the goal of improving clarity and increasing user start rate.

Full page available here.

Project Overview

Context

Company main consumer Credit Card did not have a specific landing page to direct campaign traffic.

Goal

Create a landing acquisition page with exclusively paid traffic source.

My Role

UX design, layout redesign, web development (HTML+CSS) and performance analysis.

Process

I started with a quantitative analysis of user behavior on our existing landing product pages and studied most used components and layout of competitors, identifying opportunities and key friction points. Based on these insights, I designed the layout focusing on promo offer, benefits hierarchy, readability and call-to-action visibility.

Outcome & Results

The redesign resulted in a top performer paid traffic acquisition page with a stable 12% of start rate, against 20% of organic traffic product page.

SEO Software - Project Management

I worked as Project Manager on the development of an SEO software designed to support businesses in improving their online visibility. The project covered the entire lifecycle, from market and competitor analysis to the definition of a structured business plan, followed by concept ideation and coordination between design, UX and development teams.

Project Overview

Context

The company aimed to expand its digital offering by developing an in-house SEO software in a highly competitive and saturated market.

Goal

The goal was to define a clear product concept and business strategy, ensuring alignment between market needs, user experience and technical feasibility.

My Role

  • Market and competitor analysis
  • Business plan definition
  • Product concept ideation
  • Cross-team coordination (Design, UX, Development)
  • Stakeholder alignment

Process

The project followed a structured, end-to-end approach. It started with a market and competitor analysis to understand positioning, feature gaps and differentiation opportunities within the SEO tools landscape. Based on these insights, I contributed to defining the product strategy and business plan. During the concept phase, I worked closely with the graphic design team to shape the product vision, while coordinating UX and development teams to ensure alignment between user needs, design decisions and technical constraints throughout the execution.

Outcome & Results

The project led to a well-defined and scalable SEO software concept, grounded in market insights and user-centered design principles. The structured process improved cross-team alignment and reduced friction between strategy, design and development, enabling a smoother transition from concept to implementation and setting solid foundations for future product growth.
